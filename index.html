Payment Rail Intelligence

AI-Driven Routing Engine · Multi-Rail XB Payments

CONCEPT

What this demonstrates: An AI routing layer that evaluates available payment rails for a given cross-border payment scenario — scoring each on total cost, speed, and reliability to recommend the optimal route. Rails include VD Card (VisaNet push-to-debit), VD Account / VPL (ex-Earthport bank account rail), SWIFT, and Blockchain/Crypto (e.g. Grid by Lightspark). The same routing intelligence pattern applies at any layer of the payment stack: platform-level (choosing between networks) or network-level (choosing between internal rails to the same endpoint).
